Baking cake

Baking a cake is very easy, the good thing about cakes
is that you can eat for breakfast, as dessert and as a snack.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Put 2cups of Flour mix with 1/4 cup of sugar and a spoon of baking powder
  2. Add a cup of milk and mix together
  3. Add 3 eggs
  4. Add butter
  5. After every put in the oven for 22 days lol

This is the most delicious cake ever, soft and fresh and should look like
its made out of a million bucks ingredients and it tastes like heaven.
